# ZK Reflect

Deep reflection on the Zettelkasten vault.

## Workflow

1. `zk_list` with type=permanent — all permanent notes
2. `zk_cluster_detect` — theme clusters
3. `zk_orphans` — disconnected ideas
4. Analyze: growing themes, stagnant areas, contradictions, gaps
5. Present reflection in Ukrainian — thoughtful, not mechanical
6. Suggest 3 specific next actions
